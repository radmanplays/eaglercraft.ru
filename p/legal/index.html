We ("eaglercraft.ru") are not affiliated with Minecraft.net, Mojang, or Microsoft. We provide links to the Eaglercraft version, other versions, and free trials. This website is for educational purposes only. We do not host any games on our servers nor do we condone piracy. The games shared on this website are meant for computers, except for some of the trials. If you enjoy Minecraft, you should buy the game. Our goal is to educate users on the various ways to try Minecraft for free, either through the official trials offered by the game's creators, or through custom made modifications by community members.
